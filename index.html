Welcome to my personal webpage! I'm interested in the dynamics and predictability of the weather and climate systems as well as their multiscale interactions. I received my PhD from the Pennsylvania State University working with Dr. Fuqing Zhang on the dynamics and predictability of tropical cyclones under different environmental conditions. Currently, I am a Researcher at Geophysical Institute, University of Bergen, working with Dr. Camille Li on the climate variability of atmospheric jet streams, the dynamics of high impact weather systems.

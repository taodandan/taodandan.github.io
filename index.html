Welcome to my personal webpage! I'm interested in moist convective systems, especially tropical cyclones. I received my PhD from the Pennsylvania State University working with Dr. Fuqing Zhang on the dynamics and predictability of tropical cyclones under vertical wind shear. Currently, I am a Research Scientist at the Department of Atmospheric Science, Colorado State University, working with Dr. Michael Bell and Dr. Peter Jan van Leeuwen. I'm also collaborating with Dr. Richard Rotunno at the National Center for Atmospheric Research. Recently, I am working on an axisymmetric tropical cyclone theory that better explains the steady-state tropical cyclone intensity and structure in observations and model simulations.
